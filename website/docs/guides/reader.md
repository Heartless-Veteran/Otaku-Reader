# Reader

The reader is the heart of the app — four modes, deep customization, and per-manga memory so every series opens the way it should.

![Reader menu](/screenshots/reader.png)

## Reading modes

| Mode | Best for |
|---|---|
| **Single page** | Standard manga, LTR or RTL |
| **Dual page** | Tablets and landscape — renders two-page spreads correctly |
| **Webtoon** | Long-strip webtoons, with optional auto-scroll at adjustable speed |
| **Smart Panels** | Action-dense pages — detects individual comic panels and steps through them one at a time |

Reading direction (LTR/RTL/vertical), mode, and even color filters can be **overridden per manga** — set a Korean webtoon to vertical and a Japanese series to RTL once, and the reader remembers.

## Navigation

- Tap zones: left/right edges turn pages, center opens the menu. Zones can be inverted or fully customized, with an on-screen overlay showing the layout.
- Volume keys page forward/back (hold to skip 5), with optional inversion.
- A **page slider** and **thumbnail strip** in the menu let you scrub; the **gallery view** shows the whole chapter as a grid.

## Comfort

- Brightness slider independent of system brightness.
- **Color filters**: night-friendly tints, grayscale, custom tint color, and per-manga theme overrides.
- **E-ink mode**: black-and-white rendering and full-screen flash on page turn for e-paper devices.
- Keep-screen-on, fullscreen with cutout control, secure mode (blocks screenshots), AMOLED-black background.
- **Reader presets** — save a bundle of settings (mode, filters, preload, e-ink…) and switch presets from the reader menu in one tap.

## Notes, comments, and bookmarks

- **Page bookmarks** — bookmark any page, with an optional note, and jump back from the bookmarks list.
- **Chapter notes** — one private note per chapter, visible in the chapter list.
- **Reader comments** — timestamped private comments scoped to the current chapter or to the whole series, in a panel that opens right inside the reader. Linked tracker pages (AniList, MAL, …) are one tap away if you want a public discussion.

## Under the hood

- Pages **preload** ahead and behind (configurable), and the app can **download ahead** whole chapters while you read.
- **OCR text search** can find dialogue inside pages.
- Smart prefetching adapts to your reading speed; Discord Rich Presence (optional) shows what you're reading.
